The History of Frühlingsfest

Frühlingsfest has been held anually (with a few interruptions) since 1972, and over 10,000 students have attended.

Past Frühlingsfest Sites

The first Fest was held in 1972 at MTSU, organized by the legendary Ortrun Gilbert. In recent years, it has been hosted by Sewannee, UT Chatanooga, UT Knoxville, Mount Juliet High School, and now Vanderbilt.
